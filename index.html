Australia's Import and Export trades (2016)

As consumers, sometimes we do not think where out goods are from. This visualisation will showcase Australia's imports and exports with its trading partners from all over the world. The sectors of Agriculture, forestry & fisheries, Manufactures, Minerals & fuels and other goods are also considered in the analysis.

Trades with import and export partners

Australia exports goods to Asia the most with 16,115 exports in 2016. In terms of imports, Australia imports to Europe the most with 18694 imports made in 2016.

Imports around the world

It is clear that Australia spends the most money in importing more goods from Europe and Asia. Australia spends and imports the most from China, spending approximately 59 Million AUD with 1389 imports of goods in 2016. Overall, Australia spends and trades with Africa and Oceania & Antarctica the least.

Trades by states

NSW spends the most in exporting and importing goods. This is followed by Western Australia, Victoria and Queensland. Spending is less prominent in Northern Territory, Tasmania and ACT.

Trading by sectors

It is clear that most trades include Manufactures, followed by Agriculture, forestry and fisheries. There is a vast difference in trading Minerals & fuels and other goods in each state. Australia actively trades with Asia and Europe the most
